# 📋 Command Reference

Full reference for all Aetheris Bot slash commands.

---

## 🎫 Ticket

### `/ticket`
Creates a ticket panel in the current channel. Members can click the panel to open a support ticket with a chosen category.

---

## ⭐ Leveling & Economy

### `/level rank`
View your own rank, level, and XP progress.

### `/level leaderboard`
View the server's XP leaderboard.

### `/economy daily`
Claim your daily coin reward (100–250 coins). Has a 24-hour cooldown.

### `/economy balance`
Check your current wallet and bank balance.

### `/bank deposit <amount>`
Deposit coins from your wallet into your bank.

### `/bank withdraw <amount>`
Withdraw coins from your bank into your wallet.

> 💡 Bank balance earns 2% interest automatically every Monday.

---

## 🎮 Games

### `/game dice <bet>`
Play a dice game by betting coins.

### `/game coinflip <bet> <choice>`
Bet coins on heads or tails.

### `/game slot <bet>`
Play the slot machine.

### `/game blackjack <bet>`
Play an interactive round of blackjack.

### `/game crash <bet>` *(Premium)*
Multiplier game with interactive cashout — cash out before the multiplier crashes.

---

## 🎣 Fishing & Equip

### `/fish cast`
Cast a fishing line to try catching a fish (5 rarities: Common to Legendary).

### `/fish sell <fish>`
Sell a fish from your inventory for coins.

### `/equip pancing <rod>`
Switch your equipped fishing rod to boost rare fish chances.

### `/equip umpan <bait>`
Switch your equipped bait to boost rare fish chances.

### `/equip info`
View your currently equipped rod and bait.

---

## 🏪 Shop & Inventory

### `/shop view`
Browse items available in the shop (fishing rods, bait, boosts, exclusive badges).

### `/shop buy <item>`
Purchase an item from the shop.

### `/lootbox buy <type>`
Buy a loot box (Common, Rare, or Legendary).

### `/lootbox open <id>`
Open a loot box you own for a random reward.

### `/boost use <type>`
Activate a boost (XP Boost 2x, Coin Boost 2x, or Lucky Charm — 1 hour).

### `/boost info`
View your currently active boosts.

### `/inventory`
View all items, fish, loot boxes, and badges you own.

---

## 🎒 Profile & Statistics

### `/profile view`
View your complete profile card with XP bar and badges.

### `/profile badges`
View all badges you've earned.

### `/stats view`
View your personal game and activity statistics.

### `/stats server`
View server-wide statistics.

---

## 🎉 Event & Others

### `/event info`
View the currently active server event (Double XP, Double Coins, Fishing Frenzy, or Loot Rain).

### `/roast <member>`
Roast a member with Indonesian-style sarcasm.

### `/help`
View the full list of commands, organized by category.

---

## ⭐ Premium

### `/premium redeem <key>`
Redeem a premium key to unlock premium benefits.

### `/premium status`
Check your current premium status and expiry date.

### `/gift <member> <amount>` *(Premium)*
Transfer coins to another member.

### `/history`  *(Premium)*
View your coin transaction history.
